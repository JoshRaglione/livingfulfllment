My Struggle

Addiction

I am by no means a perfect human... I have struggled with a eating disorder of bingeing and purging for more than 6 years. I wish I could give you a story of struggling with something much more stoic but some of our biggest accomplishments in life are overcoming the things that are taking away from it which in this case definitely was doing to me.

My Trigger

Pressure to be something more then I was. People held me to a high standard, alot of people in my life wanted me to do things that I would end up prusuing but later giving up because it wasnt my true self of who I wanted to be. The feeling of eating gave me a false sense of accomplishment even though I didnt know this at the time I had to do a deep self reflection of myself. I read books, articles, watched videos and even went to classes. Once I acquired all this knowledge I could see what was really causing this disorder and as I dug I found out new tools for myself and tricks that work better and better each day, each day gets easier, I cant say it was an easy road or it completely goes away but you wont look back and regret the change and if I can do it so can you!

Overcoming & Learning

I had to be a detective to find and identify my true view of myself that I was encage with I read books, took action, learned from mistakes, try new things, end unhealthy relationships, take time for myself, track myself, go to classes and through all this I have learned a lot. I brought the underlying cause out that I held on to. I then put it in a light that shows that it has no power so I could reverse, change, and live my life without feeling life doesn't favor me.
